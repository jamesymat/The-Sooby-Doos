How To Meditate for Beginners:

1. Start small and consistent - it is important to try your best to meditate frequently for the most effective results, but 3-5 minutes a day is a great place to start because it will feel like a long time when you first start.

2. Meditation is open to interpretation - you can meditate in whatever way you please: the most common way is seated, but some people find it easier to walk around a room or lie down.

3. Focus on your breating - with your crazy life, your body and mind need a break from all the chaos; basically inhale for 3 seconds, hold for 3 seconds, exhale for 3 seconds, push the rest fo your air out of your lungs, and repeat.

4. Keep your eyes closed - it is easy to get distracted by the world around you and if your eyes are closed you can visualize your inhales and exhales.

5. Repetition is key - montras or short sayings are a great way to stay focused when breathing; repeat something positive or motivational for each breath sequence.
