Healthy Recipes

What Is A Vegan Diet?

A vegan diet means that you abstain from eating any foods that have animal products.The eating well website has vegan recipes for both drinks and food for any occasion.

What Is A Vegetarian Diet?

A vegetarian diet can fall into different categories. In general, a vegetarian diet is similar to a vegan diet, in the sense that both exclude meat, poultry or fish. The Ambitious Kitchen site has 52 recipies for healthy vegetarian meals.

What Is A Gluten Free Diet?

Ceiliac Disease prevents many from consuming gluten, a protein found in grains. There are many health benefits to going gluten free, such as improving cholesterol levels. Women's Kitchen shares 42 easy to make gluten free dinner recipes.

What Is A Dairy and Lactose Free Diet?

This diet eliminates the consumption of foods that have dairy or lactose in them. It is common for people to be lactose intolerant or have a milk allergy. In case you want to make lactose or dairy free meals, eating well has all kinds of recipes from snacks to meals to desserts.

No dietary restriction? Meat Lover?

There are many ways to make your favorite foods healthier or finding healthier alternatives to what you consume. The Food Network website has over 700 recipes for all kinds of healthy dinners.
